## About Me I'm a researcher with interests in computational science, fluid dynamics, and solid mechanics. I completed my B.Tech, M.Tech, and Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering and currently work on battery modeling and multi-physics simulations at the **TESLA Group, IIT Delhi**. My research focuses on: - **Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics (SPH)** for fluid and solid mechanics - **Discrete Element Method (DEM)** for granular and particulate systems - **Coupled SPH-DEM** for fluid-structure interaction and particle dynamics - **Battery modeling** — particle swelling, multi-physics simulations Feel free to explore my [research](/research) or check out my [CV](https://drive.google.com/file/d/104Ozflq_cyvOQut92v0xvfYm0JUgO6Xn/view?usp=sharing).
